Operator Notes

Why launch pricing

These rates are intentionally set below market to build a portfolio of 5 TX events and establish planner referral relationships before increasing. Goal: raise rates 15–20% after Phase 1 is complete.

CA market entry timing

California expansion (Phase 3) begins after TX social proof is established. CA rates are 30–40% higher across all tiers to reflect market cost of living and competitor benchmarks.
